Uganda Restaurants Ordering & Reservation System

Project Overview

Project Purpose

This system allows customers to explore multiple restaurants, compare menus, order food, and reserve tables online in one place.

Objectives

  • Provide access to multiple restaurants
  • Enable online food ordering
  • Support reservations and delivery tracking
  • Improve customer convenience

Problems

  • Limited access to restaurant options
  • Manual ordering delays
  • Lack of centralized system

Causes

  • Traditional ordering systems
  • No digital integration

Solutions

  • Create a unified restaurant platform
  • Enable digital ordering & reservations
  • Provide real-time updates
